Research Fellow

IRCCS Azienda Ospedaliero Universitaria di Bologna - Policlinico di Sant’Orsola, Bologna, Italy

Within the research project "DERMO - PED: Creation of a database of anonymized images in the pediatric - dermatological field." I have been involved in two sub-tasks.

  • The first task consisted into the creation of the database anonymizing the images
  • The second task consisted into the design of the whole infrastructure needed to gather the images and anonymize it. The design has involved both technical and organizational points of view. To produce this documentation has been followed the good practice of both Software Engineering considering the impact of the infrastructure on the organization and on department processes. Have been also provided a feasibility study and a development and installation plan

Research Fellow

University of Bologna, Cesena, Italy

Within the research project "E-democracy in the times of COVID-19: elements for the construction of a digital platform for ’virtuous’ decision-making processes." I studied how the Covid-19 and the consequent usage of the digital platform into the decision-making processes has influenced to processes themselves. After a set of interviews a set of possible solutions have been proposed to address the issues emerged.
